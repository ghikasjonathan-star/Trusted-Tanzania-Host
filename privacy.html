Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Trusted Tanzania Host ("we," "us," "our"), operated by Jonathan Ghikas / Ghikas Adventures, Morogoro, Tanzania, handles information submitted through this website.

1. Information We Collect

When you use our contact/intake form, we collect the information you choose to provide: name, email address, phone/WhatsApp number, client type, area of interest, budget range, timeline, and any message content ("Mission Briefing"). We do not use cookies or tracking scripts beyond standard, anonymised website analytics if enabled.

2. How the Form Works

Our intake form does not transmit data to a server. Submitting the form opens your own email application with the information pre-filled, addressed to info@ghikasadventures.com. You choose whether to send that email. Alternatively, you may contact us directly via WhatsApp.

3. How We Use Your Information

Information you send us is used solely to respond to your enquiry, assess fit for our services, and, if you proceed, to prepare a Service Agreement. We do not sell or share your information with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Data Retention

We retain enquiry and client information only as long as reasonably necessary for the purposes above, or as required by Tanzanian law.

5. Third-Party Links

This site links to Ghikas Adventures and Luminara Gems, and to third-party platforms such as WhatsApp and LinkedIn. Each has its own privacy practices, which we encourage you to review.

6. Your Choices

You may request that we delete your enquiry information at any time by contacting info@ghikasadventures.com.

7. Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to info@ghikasadventures.com or via WhatsApp.
