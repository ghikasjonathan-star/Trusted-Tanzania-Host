01 — Charity & NGO Logistics

Get your mission moving without losing months to local unknowns.

Scope I coordinate access and logistics. Formal registration, legal compliance and fund governance remain the responsibility of your organisation and its own counsel.
  • Vetted introductions to community leaders and relevant local authorities
  • Permit and community-entry protocol coordination
  • Bilingual translation (English / Swahili) for meetings and materials
  • Ground transport, accommodation and safety planning for field visits
  • Ongoing local point-of-contact between visits
02 — Investment & Business Support

See the real conditions on the ground before you commit capital.

Scope I am not a licensed financial advisor or broker. Site visits and coordination only — always verify structures with independent, licensed counsel.
  • Site visits and first-hand condition reports for prospective ventures
  • Navigation support through local registration processes (BRELA-adjacent guidance)
  • Coordination with independent lawyers, accountants and licensed brokers
  • Due-diligence logistics — introductions, translation, document collection
  • Monthly remote retainer available for absentee investors
03 — Real Estate Guidance

Tour properties and premises with someone who reads the area, not just the listing.

Scope I am not a licensed real-estate broker or notary. I coordinate viewings and area context — title verification should always be confirmed independently.
  • Guided property, apartment, office and premises tours
  • Area and neighbourhood analysis by region
  • Coordination with independent surveyors and licensed brokers for title checks
  • Photo/video documentation of properties for absentee buyers
  • Ongoing local point-of-contact after purchase or lease
04 — Mineral Fields & Gemstone Ventures

Local, ethical orientation across Tanzania's gemstone-producing regions.

Important Local orientation and context only. I do not broker mining claims, mineral rights, or investment contracts. For gemstone sourcing and trade, visit our sister company Luminara Gems ↗.
  • Local orientation and context for gemstone-producing regions (e.g. Tanzanite-producing areas, Mahenge)
  • Local context, safety planning and respectful community protocol
  • Introductions to licensed dealers via Luminara Gems for anything transactional
  • Photo/video documentation for remote stakeholders
05 — Explorer & Relocation Support

Settling in or passing through — either way, you're not on your own.

Scope Practical, local support — not immigration or legal representation. Visa and residency matters should go through a licensed immigration professional.
  • Long-stay accommodation sourcing and orientation
  • Local SIM, banking and day-to-day logistics guidance
  • Introductions to trusted local contacts and services
  • Ongoing point-of-contact for anything unexpected
