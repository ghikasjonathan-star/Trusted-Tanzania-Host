This page describes the scope of services provided by Trusted Tanzania Host ("we," "us," "our"), operated by Jonathan Ghikas / Ghikas Adventures, based in Morogoro, Tanzania. By engaging our services, you acknowledge and agree to the terms below.

1. Nature of Our Services

Trusted Tanzania Host provides on-the-ground logistics, coordination, guidance, translation, and introduction services to investors, NGOs, diaspora professionals, and independent explorers operating in Tanzania. We facilitate access and coordinate practical arrangements — we do not perform the underlying legal, financial, or regulated professional functions ourselves.

2. We Are Not Licensed Professionals in These Areas

  • We are not a law firm and do not provide legal advice.
  • We are not a licensed real estate broker or notary, and do not verify land title on your behalf as a matter of legal record.
  • We are not a licensed financial advisor, broker, or investment company, and nothing we say or write constitutes financial or investment advice.
  • We do not broker mining claims, mineral rights, or gemstone investment contracts. Mineral-field engagements are limited to guided, ethical site visits.

You are strongly advised to engage independent, licensed legal counsel, a registered surveyor, a licensed broker, and/or a qualified financial advisor before entering into any binding transaction, contract, land purchase, investment, or mining-related agreement in Tanzania.

3. No Liability for Third-Party Transactions

Trusted Tanzania Host facilitates access, introductions, and logistics but is not a party to, and holds no liability for, the outcome of any transaction, contract, investment, or agreement entered into by the client with a third party (including landowners, sellers, government offices, mining-region contacts, or other local parties we may introduce you to).

4. Signed Service Agreement Required

No on-ground engagement, site visit, or paid service begins without a signed Service Agreement between the client and Trusted Tanzania Host, outlining the specific scope, fees, and responsibilities of each party for that engagement. Enquiry forms and initial WhatsApp/email discussions do not constitute a binding agreement.

Any engagement related to mineral-producing regions or gemstones is strictly limited to guided, ethical site visits and local context. Sourcing, trading, or investment-related gemstone transactions are handled separately by our sister company, Luminara Gems, under its own terms and legal footing.

Clients are responsible for their own compliance with Tanzanian law, including but not limited to business registration, tax obligations, immigration/visa requirements, land law, and mining regulations.
