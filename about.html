The Anchor

Born in Morogoro. Trained as a scientist. Licensed as a guide.

I'm Jonathan Gregory Ghikas — a Tanzanian wildlife scientist, TTLB-licensed guide, and founder of Ghikas Adventures. Born and raised in Morogoro, I built Trusted Tanzania Host because too many investors, NGOs, and diaspora professionals arrive here and lose months (and money) to unreliable middlemen.

Every engagement is handled personally. I don't oversell what I can deliver, and I tell you plainly when something is outside my lane — which is exactly why clients keep coming back.
